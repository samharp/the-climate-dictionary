Foreward

Climate change is without doubt the defining global issue of our time. It is already affecting every country around the globe. But many of the terms and concepts relating to climate change can be complicated and inaccessible.

There is a need for accurate, easy-to-understand information about climate change and related topics.

The following dictionary is a collection of definitions for frequently used climate terms, written simply.

If you have ever questioned 'What does Adaptation mean?', 'What is the difference between Carbon removal and Carbon capture?' or similar, this dictionary will help you to understand.
